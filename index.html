About Me

I started coding by self learning Visual Basic at the age of 10. My love for software only grew from there, learning HTML, Java and C in my personal time. Spent my college years learning theory at WPI, including a year of Grad School there before I started my career at Savant Systems. At Savant I worked mostly in Objective-C and C while I worked on both Host/Runtime side and the Setup/Design portion of our software. After helping design many User Interface's, I started working on iOS apps using Swift, UIKit and SwiftUI.

After Savant, I joined Barwave where we have a Social Media/Find your next bar app using SwiftUI with a complex integration of UIKits MapKit and SwiftUI pop-ups. My personal projects have mostly been SwiftUI, as have my freelance work (which of course will not be shown here). Recently, I have begun to learn newer HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Android. Currently, leaning into AI to help me produce at a faster rate than I could type manually while learning the intricacies of prompt engineering.
